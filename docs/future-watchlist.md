# Future Watchlist

This page is for **moving forward**. It is not a history page.

The goal is to keep a live watchlist of the events and source streams most likely to change the `2026 -> 2028` architecture story.

Use `GTC 2026` materials as the current baseline for architecture interpretation, but do not list `GTC 2026` itself as a future event.

## Highest-Priority Future Events

### COMPUTEX

- Why watch it: OEM systems, partner announcements, racks, platforms, supply chain signals.
- What to look for: `Rubin`, `Vera`, networking partners, liquid-cooled systems, rack-scale packaging.

### ISC

- Why watch it: HPC and AI infrastructure, energy efficiency, cluster design, networking, facility constraints.
- What to look for: benchmark disclosures, scale-out fabric discussion, energy and cooling commentary.

### Hot Chips

- Why watch it: deepest public architecture talks.
- What to look for: chip packaging, memory systems, interconnect details, CPU-GPU integration.

### OCP Global Summit

- Why watch it: racks, power, cooling, facility design, open hardware.
- What to look for: megawatt-to-gigawatt buildout clues, rack power density, cooling architectures.

### SC

- Why watch it: system-scale deployments, supercomputing, networking, large-cluster design.
- What to look for: real systems, topologies, power numbers, cluster performance claims.
